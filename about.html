about me


I'm a designer-cum-developer based in windy Bengaluru, India.


I am a fresh graduate who has a deep interest in UI/UX Design as well as Full Stack Development. Apart from designing and coding, I like to read non-fiction books, watch or play cricket, and also I've been trying to 'stay in the moment' recently.

I believe that the beauty of building a software lies in the fact that it could impact billions of lives. I aim to become a 'jack-of-all-trades-master-of-none', and would call myself a problem-solver rather than a programmer.
